Paneer

Description

Paneer is a tasty recipe made up of milk. Paneer is white in colour and found in every state of india.
To make paneer first mix some drops of lemon in milk and after it heat the milk and then pack it with a cloth tightly. After some time the panner is ready.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Cashews
  • Cream

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add in sliced onions and saute for 2 mins
  2. Add in tomatoes.
  3. Add in cashews.
  4. Cook till it turns mushy
  5. Take it in a blender and puree till smooth
  6. Add in 1 tbsp butter.
  7. Add in cumin seeds.
